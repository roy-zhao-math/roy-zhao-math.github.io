Caltech Number Theory Learning Seminar: Lawrence–Venkatesh Method

Spring 2024

This is the website for the Caltech Number Theory Learning Seminar. This is a weekly seminar intended to understand Lawrence and Venkatesh's proof of the Mordell Conjecture. Each week, a speaker will present on a topic. Here is the outline (PDF).
